" collection: portfolio --- Ultrasounds are a widely used medical diagnostic tool, but many communities in the world have limited access to the expertise required to conduct an ultrasound. This is why I focused my thesis on the topic of remote ultrasounds, and how to improve them. It was hypothesised that the combination of a commercially available haptic feedback system combined with a commercially available Robot would both improve the precision of remote ultrasounds and enable the technology to be more widespread.

This is a project in progress.





Experiences Gained: ROS (Robot Operating System) proficiency, Literature Reviews and Research, Homogeneous Transformations, Inverse Kinematics, Jacobian Matrices, Robot Trajectories, UR5e usage
